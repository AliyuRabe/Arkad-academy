Our Classes levels

Muna da Matakan Kwarewa Rukuni Ukku domin farawa

Beginner

Matakin Farawa na nufin wanda ya fara koyon wani aiki, fasaha, ko wani fanni na karatu. Matakin Farawa yawanci ba su da kwarewa sosai, kuma suna cikin matakin farko na koyon abu ko yin amfani da shi. Matakin Farawa yawanci suna neman ilimin asali, umarni masu sauki, da jagoranci don habaka kwarewarsu da kwarin guiwa a hankali domin koyo.

Intermediate

Matakin Matsakaici na nufin wanda ya wuce matakin farko (farawa) a cikin wata fasaha, aiki, ko fanni, amma bai kai matakin gaba (na ci gaba) ba. Yana da fahimta mai kyau game da abubuwan asali kuma zai iya yin ayyuka da wani matakin kwarewa. Duk da haka, har yanzu yana bukatar kara Kwarewa, koyon karin dabaru masu zurfi, ko samun karin Kwarewa.

Advance

Matakin Kwarewa (Gaba) yana nufin wanda yake ta gogewa a wani abu, kamar a karatu wanda yake mai wannan matakin yafi saurin fahimtar abubuwa saboda yana ilimin sharar fage karin haske kadan ya wadatar ya fashimci wani ilimi. Mai Matakin Kwarewa zai iyin mentorship, leadership and guide other to archive their goals.
